About Us





Where Are We Located?

We are located in New York City, near Times Square on West 43rd Street. This is the perfect location if you are walking around the city in Midtown, visiting for a Broadway Show, or any other occasion.

What Type of Cuisine Do We Serve?

Eric Zorn's Pizzeria has been serving up the finest New York style pizza pies since the early 1980's. We have a pie for everyone. We use only the finest Italian ingredients that are imported from Italy every day. Our most famous pie is definitely the standard tomato pie that has fresh mozzarella, homemade tomato sauce, and basil. The pie is topped with our fresh extra-virgin olive oil.
